Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Visualizations

Similar in one way to the humidity visualization, this visualization of the cloudiness on a specific day in 500+ randomly selected cities across the globe shows no obvious correlation between cloudiness and latitude. It does however show clusters of cloudiness around the 75% and the 90% cloudiness percentages. Further research will need to be done to determine the cause of these clusters.
